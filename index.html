FROM ORGANIC WASTE

At Saaf, bio-methanization means much more than the production of biogas during the anaerobic digestion of organic matter. In fact it tells us a lot about the living standards of our communities, cutting across universal development themes of environment, health, energy access, mobility, soil & water quality and climate change, not to mention touching upon age old social hierarchies of caste surrounding waste handling in India. At Saaf we believe that cleaning up our landfills is about cleaning up our act all round as a society and building a biogas plant is about building livable, sustainable and energy secure cities that borrow from the past, present and future.
